# DS-002 — Platform Mark

## Status

Approved

## Version

1.0

## Purpose

The Brian Short Apps Platform Mark is the primary visual identity of the Brian Short Apps ecosystem.

Unlike a traditional logo, the Platform Mark is both:

* a brand identity
* an interactive system component

It communicates the relationship between the platform, its products, and continued progress.

---

# Philosophy

The Platform Mark represents an ecosystem.

Applications do not exist independently.

They exist inside a shared engineering platform that provides consistency, reliability, and a familiar user experience.

The Platform Mark visualizes that relationship.

---

# Ring Model

## Outer Ring

### Experience

Represents the shared Brian Short Apps user experience.

Every product should feel immediately familiar.

---

## Middle Ring

### Products

Represents the family of Brian Short Apps products.

Products may evolve independently while remaining connected to the platform.

---

## Inner Ring

### Progress

Represents continuous improvement.

Technology evolves.

Products improve.

The platform continues moving forward.

---

# Center Product Zone

The center circle belongs to the application.

The Platform Mark exists to support the product.

The product remains the primary focus.

---

# Interaction Model

## Default State

The ring breaks intentionally do **not** align.

This represents:

* independent domains
* separate responsibilities
* distributed engineering

The platform is working together, but not yet focused on a single task.

---

## Interactive State

On hover, touch, or focus:

The ring breaks align.

Alignment creates a clear visual path to the center product zone.

This communicates:

* clarity
* focus
* access
* purposeful interaction

The Platform Mark becomes an experience rather than a static image.

---

# Ring Geometry

## Outer Ring

Breaks:

* 12 o'clock
* 4 o'clock
* 8 o'clock

---

## Middle Ring

Default:

* 10 o'clock
* 2 o'clock
* 6 o'clock

Interactive:

Rotates into alignment with the outer and inner rings.

---

## Inner Ring

Breaks:

* 12 o'clock
* 4 o'clock
* 8 o'clock

The inner ring intentionally shares the outer ring's geometry.

---

# Motion Principles

Motion communicates.

Motion never decorates.

Animation should:

* remain calm
* remain deliberate
* reinforce access
* reinforce focus

The animation should never distract from the product.

---

# Component Model

The Platform Mark is implemented as a reusable Design System component.

Applications consume the component rather than creating their own implementations.

This ensures:

* consistent geometry
* consistent interaction
* consistent motion
* consistent accessibility

---

# Accessibility

The Platform Mark must:

* remain understandable without animation
* preserve keyboard accessibility
* expose meaningful labels to assistive technologies
* maintain sufficient contrast

Motion should always respect reduced-motion preferences.

---

# Future Evolution

The Platform Mark is expected to evolve through future Design System versions.

Future enhancements may include:

* configurable center product content
* configurable interaction behaviors
* accessibility refinements
* additional motion states

The underlying philosophy should remain stable.
